FAQ

What services does XPuzzle provide? +

XPuzzle is an easy-to-use trading aggregator supporting multiple CLOB PerpDEXs. Trade spot and perpetual contracts including crypto, stocks, forex, gold, and oil.

We automatically select the best DEX with lowest slippage and fees based on your trading intent.

XPuzzle also detects trading mining incentives and supports custom trading bots and AI agents for automated trading.

How to deposit assets to XPuzzle? +

Transfer tokens to XPuzzle deposit addresses on Arbitrum and BNB Chain.

We will add support for ETH, Base, Optimism, Polygon, Avalanche, Injective, Solana, XLayer, and Monad in the future.

Is there a XPuzzle Mobile APP? Where can I get it? +

Mobile app will launch in Q2 2026. Download links will be available on our website.

What login methods does XPuzzle support? +

XPuzzle supports Web3 wallets like MetaMask, and social logins including phone, email, Twitter, and Google accounts.

Multi-factor authentication is available with automatic detection of suspicious logins.
